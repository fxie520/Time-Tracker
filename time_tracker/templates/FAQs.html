Q: What are the rules?

A:

  • A stage contains multiple days, while a day belongs to one single stage.
    • When a stage is deleted, all days belong to it are deleted as well.
    • Total usable time of stage = sum of usable time of days belonging to the stage
    • Total study time of stage = sum of study time of days belonging to the stage
    • Time usage ratio of stage (0 - 1) = total study time of stage / total usable time of stage
  • A day contains multiple sessions, while a session belongs to one single day.
    • When a day is deleted, all sessions belong to it are deleted as well.
    • Worktime of day = time spent inevitably for education/living purpose (contrary to personal growth)
    • Start time of day = the time you get up
    • End time of day = the time you go to bed, which may surpass midnight
    • Usable time of day = end time of day - start time of day - worktime of day
    • Study time of day = sum of duration of sessions belonging to the day
    • Time usage ratio of day (0 - 1) = study time of day / usable time of day
  • A session belongs to one single day, and one single subject.
    • Start time of session = the time you start learning
    • End time of session = the time you stop learning
    • Duration of session = end time of session - start time of session
  • A subject contains multiple sessions, while a session belongs to one single subject.
    • When a subject is deleted, all sessions belong to it are deleted as well.
    • Total study time of subject = sum of duration of sessions belonging to the subject
